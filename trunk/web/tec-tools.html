MODNLP: tec-tools: tec-client/tec-server

TEC (short for "Translational English Corpus") is a computerised collection of contemporary translational English text. The TEC processing software (tec-client and tec-server) were originally developed to allow free access to the corpus over the Internet. These tools are currently being revamped and integrated into modnlp and will soon be made available on this site. The old distributions of TEC are available (under the GPL) at the TEC software web site, where you can also try the concordancer (via Java Web Start).

More information on the TEC client/server architecture can be found in the following paper:

[1] S. Luz. A software toolkit for sharing and accessing corpora over the Internet. In M. Gavrilidou, G. Carayannis, S. Markantonatou, S. Piperidis, and G. Stainhauer, editors, Proceedings of the Second International Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation: LREC-2000, pages 1749-1754, May 2000.
